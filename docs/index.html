We feel that we have made decent progress relative to our planned deliverables. First, we installed and learned OpenGL, which we feel was a major hurdle in becoming familiar with the tools we would need to initialize our project. Depicted below is one step in the tutorial: displaying a window.


Then, we made textures for some blocks, which would be part of our deliverables, with the ability to view it from all angles by rotating the camera.


Finally, we applied the textures to blocks, for which rendering them facing the right way was a challenge, and built a plane with said blocks.

So far, things appear to look good and as intended. In other news, we are working on lighting/shading with a stationary light source, and detecting block collisions. We feel that for now, we should be on track to deliver what we planned, though we expect to have difficulties regarding the physics/handling of block collisions and destroying/placing blocks. The textures are also quite glitchy when moving the camera quickly, which seems like a difficult obstacle to overcome.
